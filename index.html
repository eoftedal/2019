Code of conduct

BSides Oslo is a safe and inclusive community based conference, aimed at sharing information security knowledge. Speakers, participants, organizers and voulenteers are required to read and adhere to the code of conduct.

About

Security BSides is a global network of independent community-driven and inclusive information security events. The first Security BSides in Oslo will be held on May 23rd, 2019 at MESH (Tordenskiolds gate 3), in the middle of central Oslo.

We've already gotten a lot of support from the local community, including several sponsors. We've announced our speakers announcing speakers, and also posted our program.

The BSidesOslo Team

The BSidesOslo team are members of the local infosec community who felt the need for an accessible, inclusive event that is focused on learning and sharing. The BSides framework is a perfect fit. While individual BSides events have a focus on serving and representing their local communities, adopting this framework also ties us to an international network of diverse, independent events that seek to create opportunities to advance the infosec conversation beyond the mainstream.
